About Us

Viking Strategic Metals Inc is a mineral exploration company whose focus is finding mineral deposits of sufficient size and grade to justify further development and feed material into the current strategic metals markets and help in the development of applications into sustaining “green” technologies.

US Focus

As the world continues to make scientific advancements, the production and supply of strategic metals will become the limiting factor in their implementation into society unless new deposits are discovered in jurisdictions that encourage mining and protect the rule of law.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to create shareholder value through pursuit and development of a mineralogically diverse portfolio of exploration projects located in countries where rule of law is valued and upheld. Interest in strategic metals has never been greater and we look to capitalize on that interest by providing a vehicle to explore for strategic minerals including: vanadium, cobalt, copper, nickel, and tungsten. To this end we have acquired several high-grade projects across the strategic metals spectrum. The Viking managerial team covers all facets of exploration including: geology, legal, managerial, and in the field data collection, modeling, and analysis. Our expertise focuses on being able to identify low-cost, high-yield projects as we aim to take early, green-field projects and conduct the geochemical, geophysical and drilling work required to aggressively progress our projects along the development pathway.
